Project 9

In this open world first person shooter you are a person that survived a global experiment that went wrong: Project 9. Learn more about the Story, world and their inhabitants...

Story

Before a terrible accident in experiment number 9 wiped out almost the entire population, you lived a perfectly normal life. Now you have to fight every day for bare survival.

World

There is much to discover in the world of "Project 9". Besides dense forests and beautiful nature, there are small villages but also a big city to discover.

People

You will meet numerous people. Some will become your allies, others will become bitter enemies who only want you dead.
